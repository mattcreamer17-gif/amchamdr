# AMCHAMDR pitch page

A single-file, bilingual (Spanish-primary, English parity) landing page prepared for AMCHAMDR's Technology and Innovation Committee and the office of the Executive Vice President. It frames the natural next step after the QVITAE youth AI program: an executive cohort for the chamber's 1,600+ member companies, with Eduba as the methodology partner and AMCHAMDR in the convening role it already plays. The page uses only the chamber's own brand tokens (navy #00457B, red #C8102E, Open Sans, rectilinear chamber layout with a red-bridge rule) and closes with a thirty-minute call slot on Matt Creamer's calendar.
